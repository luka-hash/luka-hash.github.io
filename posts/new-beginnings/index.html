New beginnings

August 21, 2023

Welcome

I’ve been planning to make a blog for a while now - so I just decided to stop procrastinating and do it. So… Welcome!

What to expect?

Ramblings of a random internet person, I guess. The main intention of this blog is to have a place to share my ramblings on technology. But I can not promise there won’t be some random posts here and there.

Who am I?

Well… I’m Luka.

Right now, I’m studying mathematics, but technology in general is what I’ve been doing my whole life. I’m a generalist - I like doing systems programming as well as web development. But I also like doing non-tech things.

It is kind of hard for me to give you a few-sentence summary of myself, so I’ll just stop here and leave you to roam this blog and find out for yourself.

In case you want to contact me, you can send me a mail at luka [at] ljudi [dot] org. You can also see some of the things I’m working on here.

Cheers!
